The Location

18 kms from Panaji, Anjuna beach is part of a 30 km extended coastline on the west of Goa. Famous for its stunning natural beauty, swaying palms and pristine white sands, it is an ideal place to escape into the boundless beauty of nature. The blissful atmosphere, luminous full-moon and blue water cast a spell that make it the perfect place for a new home.

Robert Greg Sand


The architect & Designer

THE MIND BEHIND THE PERFECTION

With over 20 years of experience as an architect, the mind behind the concept design of our luxury villas has been consulting and working on projects in various parts of the world, especially in the Asia Pacific region. His wide travel has given him a unique perspective on design that marries the Japanese philosophy of ‘less-is-more’ with Western values. A member of the Singapore Board of Architects, Shand conceptualised III at Anjuna as bespoke sustainable luxury homes.
